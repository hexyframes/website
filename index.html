Pyrolysis Machine

Pyrolysis is an advanced recycling method that uses heat in an oxygen-free environment to break down plastic waste into valuable products like fuel oil, gas, and solid char. Unlike traditional incineration, it prevents burning and instead performs a thermal decomposition to create reusable materials and fuels without producing ash or harmful emissions.
